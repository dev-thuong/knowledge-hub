# Tag `#system`

<details>
<summary>What is the #system tag?</summary>

<br>

The **#system** tag is used to mark content related to the **overall system** within KnowledgeHub.

Its scope includes:

* Overall architecture
* Main components and their roles
* System operating principles
* Workflows and interactions between different parts

Articles with this tag serve a **guiding role**, helping to ensure:

* A clear high-level overview
* Coherence between components
* Scalability and long-term evolution
* A correct understanding of how the system functions as a unified whole
